My Story

I have been a learner my entire life.

In school, any subject that challenged me became my favorite. The material never mattered as much as the opportunity to

grow.

When I worked in finance, our parent company discontinued the CRM we used for years.


As A Result:

We were faced with a short-term G A P in the flow of business-critical info.

That gap was a

PROBLEM.

Life is about

SOLUTIONS.

That problem became an opportunity for me to build an interactive Excel table to FILL the gap.

That spreadsheet became a full-blown
LOVE AFFAIR
with creating tools that look good and get the job done.

As I've pushed toward aligning passion with profession, I've pursued every possible avenue to learn the industry:

Crafting full-stack projects to meet needs in my own life and get my hands on myriad languages and frameworks.
